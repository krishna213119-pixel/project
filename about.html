About the Club

College Coding Club is a community of students who enjoy building real-world projects, solving coding challenges, and learning new technologies together.

The club regularly conducts sessions on programming fundamentals, algorithms, competitive coding, and web development.

Members also collaborate on mini projects involving Arduino, IoT, and basic electronics to connect hardware with software.

Our goal is to create a supportive environment where students can ask doubts freely, share resources, and improve their problem-solving skills.

Through events, workshops, and peer learning, the club prepares students for internships, hackathons, and technical interviews.
