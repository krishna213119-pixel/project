Events & Activities

These are some of the core events conducted by the College Coding Club during the semester.

Web Development Workshop

An introductory workshop covering HTML, CSS, and basic layout techniques to build your first website.

Data Structures Bootcamp

An intensive series of sessions focused on arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, and time complexity analysis.

Arduino & IoT Session

A hands-on activity where students use Arduino boards and sensors to build simple automation projects.
